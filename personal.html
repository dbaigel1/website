Being the child of two South African immigrants to America, traveling has always been a valued part of our lives. Over the past few years I have gone to some incredible countries and luckily had the foresight to jot down my thoughts while I was there. Most recently I've spent a year teaching English at a primary school in Shenzhen, China. As I begin to wrap up my traveling experience in Asia and head back home to the states, I decided that I needed a place to gather all my experiences together. More importantly, I'll be able to add entries for new countries I travel to in the future. Thanks for stopping by!
